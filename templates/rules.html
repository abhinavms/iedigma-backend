RULES



Indus Enigma is a 24 hr Treasure hunt cum Hackathon. Solve all the levels within the stipulated time limit. Be the first and take home the prize.



How to play.

  • Start the game by entering the first level via the link.
  • You will be presented with a clue or puzzle which will lead you to the next level.
  • There can be multiple clues and hidden clues within the site.
  • Find the solution and follow the instructions given (if any)
    • or
    Change the last part of the url with the solution, if there are no instructions
  • Try all possible answers in the url. There is no Capital letters or spacing in the answers.
  • Eg: if your answer is “Tomato Potato” Enter your answer as tomatopotato at the end of the url
  • Url is located at the address bar.
    Please note:
  • The game is recommended to be played on a PC or Laptop with good internet connection. Mobile users may find difficulties in certain levels so try avoiding mobile phones.
  • The game is meant to be played by a single player no teams are allowed.
  • Please avoid sharing answers and posting spoilers on the internet.
  • The first person to complete all the challenges within the stipulated time will be deemed the winner
  • We are not responsible for the speed of internet connection or any other liabilities that can be caused by playing the game.
  • Organizers reserve the right to disqualify any contestant for malpractice, if found
  • In case of any dispute the decision of the organizers will be final


Happy playing, may the best win.
